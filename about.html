I am a rising Senior (4th year) studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of Notre Dame, where I am a Sorin Scholar.

I enjoy the pursuit of sophisticated — yet elegant — solutions to mechanical design problems. I seek to apply my knowledge of materials, manufacturing, and design to build wonderful things.

I have already had some incredible opportunities to do this in the past — I am currently interning at Pratt & Whitney as a manufacturing engineer in the additive manufacturing R&D lab. I have previously interned at nuTonomy (now Motional) in Singapore and worked as an undergraduate researcher at the Innovative Robotics and Interactive Systems Lab (IRIS Lab) at Notre Dame.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and listening to a wide variety of music. I am currently in the process of rediscovering my love for playing the piano.
